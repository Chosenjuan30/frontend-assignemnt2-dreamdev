Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I apply for a programme at TechBridge?

You can apply online via our website at www.techbridge.edu.ng, visit any of our three campuses in person, or call our admissions hotline on +234 (0)12-345-6789 to speak with an admissions adviser.

Are scholarships available for prospective students?

Yes. TechBridge administers several scholarship and bursary schemes, including the Women in Tech Scholarship, the Merit Excellence Award, and the Corporate Bursary Programme. Details and eligibility criteria are available on our website. You may also submit a scholarship enquiry using the form on this page.

Does TechBridge offer online or distance learning?

Yes. Our online learning division provides fully accredited programmes delivered through the TechBridge Learning Management System (TBLMS). Online students receive the same quality of instruction as campus students, including live virtual classes, recorded lectures, project mentorship, and career support.

Can my organisation partner with TechBridge for staff training?

Absolutely. We work with organisations of all sizes to design bespoke training programmes, executive workshops, and professional certification pathways. Please select "Corporate training partnership" from the enquiry type in the form above, or email partnerships@techbridge.edu.ng.

What are the entry requirements for diploma programmes?

Entry requirements vary by programme level. Foundation programmes require a minimum of five O'level credits including Mathematics and English Language. Advanced diploma programmes typically require a relevant first degree or HND. Full requirements for each programme are listed on our admissions page.
