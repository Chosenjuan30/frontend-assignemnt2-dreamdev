People & structure

Our organisation

TechBridge Institute is powered by a dedicated team of educators, researchers, and administrative professionals committed to delivering transformative learning experiences.

Leadership

Management team

Meet the experienced leaders who guide TechBridge Institute's strategy, operations, and academic excellence.

AO

Prof. Afolabi Okonkwo, PhD

Executive Director & Co-Founder

Professor Okonkwo holds a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Lagos and an MBA from Cranfield University, United Kingdom. With over 25 years of combined academic and industry experience, he founded TechBridge in 2009 with a vision to democratise technology education in West Africa. He sits on the boards of the Information Technology Developers Association of Nigeria (ITAN) and the ECOWAS Digital Skills Task Force.

CE

Dr. Chioma Eze

Deputy Director, Academics

Dr. Eze leads all academic matters, programme development, and quality assurance. A Cambridge-educated curriculum designer with 18 years of experience in higher education, she is passionate about student-centred learning and inclusive pedagogy.

FB

Ms. Fatima Bello

Director, Research & Innovation

Ms. Bello oversees TechBridge's research agenda and industry collaboration partnerships. She previously led the digital transformation programme at Access Bank and holds an MSc in Technology Management from UCL.

TA

Mr. Tunde Adeyemi, ICAN

Director, Finance & Operations

A chartered accountant with extensive non-profit and education sector experience, Mr. Adeyemi manages budgeting, procurement, facilities, and all operational functions across the institute's three campuses.

GA

Mrs. Grace Adeola

Director, Student Affairs

Mrs. Adeola champions student well-being, career services, and alumni engagement. She established TechBridge's award-winning mentorship scheme, which has connected over 4,000 students with industry professionals.

EN

Dr. Emeka Nwosu

Head, Software Development Unit

Dr. Nwosu oversees TechBridge's flagship software development programmes. A full-stack engineer and certified AWS architect, he ensures students graduate with industry-grade technical skills and strong portfolios.

AK

Dr. Amina Kazeem

Head, Data Science & AI Unit

Dr. Kazeem brings deep expertise in machine learning, statistical modelling, and business intelligence. She leads TechBridge's AI research lab and has published extensively in international data science journals.

Departments

Units & their activities

TechBridge operates six specialist units, each contributing distinct expertise to the institute's educational mission.

Academics & Training Unit

Unit Head: Dr. Emeka Nwosu  |  Staff: 42 lecturers, 8 lab technicians

  • Designing and delivering undergraduate and postgraduate diploma programmes in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and digital entrepreneurship.
  • Reviewing and updating curricula bi-annually in partnership with industry advisory boards to ensure relevance and competitiveness.
  • Coordinating examinations, assessments, and academic integrity processes across all campuses and online platforms.
  • Managing laboratory facilities, equipment maintenance schedules, and software licence procurement.
  • Providing professional development workshops and short courses for working professionals and corporate clients.
  • Running the lecturer development programme to enhance teaching quality and pedagogical innovation.

Research & Innovation Unit

Unit Head: Ms. Fatima Bello  |  Staff: 12 researchers, 4 innovation leads

  • Conducting applied technology research addressing challenges in healthcare, agriculture, finance, and public administration across West Africa.
  • Publishing peer-reviewed papers, case studies, and policy briefs in collaboration with national and international academic institutions.
  • Managing the TechBridge Innovation Lab, which provides co-working space, prototyping equipment, and mentorship to student entrepreneurs.
  • Administering the annual TechSolve Hackathon, attracting over 600 participants from across Nigeria and neighbouring countries.
  • Facilitating technology transfer agreements and intellectual property registrations for commercially viable student inventions.

Finance & Operations Unit

Unit Head: Mr. Tunde Adeyemi  |  Staff: 18 finance staff, 22 facilities staff

  • Preparing and managing the institute's annual budget of over ₦2.4 billion, ensuring fiscal discipline and transparency.
  • Overseeing procurement, asset management, and vendor relationships for all campuses and online infrastructure.
  • Managing payroll, staff benefits administration, and compliance with statutory financial obligations.
  • Maintaining and developing campus facilities, including classrooms, laboratories, hostels, and common areas.
  • Coordinating health and safety compliance, security services, and business continuity planning.
  • Producing quarterly and annual financial reports for the Board of Trustees and regulatory authorities.

Student Affairs Unit

Unit Head: Mrs. Grace Adeola  |  Staff: 14 counsellors, 10 career advisers

  • Providing academic counselling, mental health support, and pastoral care to all enrolled students.
  • Operating the Career Services Centre, including CV clinics, interview preparation workshops, and employer engagement events.
  • Administering the TechBridge Scholarship Fund, identifying and supporting financially disadvantaged students with merit-based awards.
  • Coordinating student clubs, societies, and extracurricular technology competitions such as hackathons and coding challenges.
  • Managing the alumni network of over 12,400 graduates, facilitating mentorship matching and networking events.
  • Overseeing student accommodation, transport, and on-campus welfare services across all three campuses.

ICT Services Unit

Unit Head: Mr. Kayode Sule  |  Staff: 16 engineers, 6 support analysts

  • Maintaining and upgrading the institute's network infrastructure, servers, and cloud computing environments across three campuses.
  • Developing and supporting the TechBridge Learning Management System (TBLMS), used by over 2,500 active learners.
  • Ensuring cybersecurity compliance, data protection, and system uptime through proactive monitoring and incident response.
  • Providing first-line and second-line technical support to staff and students, resolving hardware and software issues.
  • Evaluating and integrating emerging educational technologies, including virtual reality labs and AI-powered tutoring tools.

Marketing & Communications Unit

Unit Head: Ms. Ngozi Osei  |  Staff: 9 marketing staff, 4 designers

  • Developing and executing integrated marketing campaigns to attract prospective students domestically and internationally.
  • Managing the institute's website, social media platforms, and digital advertising accounts to maintain strong online presence.
  • Producing printed and digital publications including the TechBridge Annual Report, programme brochures, and the quarterly newsletter.
  • Coordinating press relations, media interviews, and the institute's participation in education fairs and industry conferences.
  • Planning and executing institutional events including the annual Convocation Ceremony, Open Days, and the TechBridge Technology Summit.
